Best Places in the Philippines

Cebu City

  • Walk around the second major metropolitan area in the Philippines this Christmas!
  • Cebu City has many things to offer, from local food to historical Christian landmarks


Boracay

  • One of the Best Visayas Tourist spots, Boracay is known for it's powdery white sand and clear waters.
  • Swim in one of the best beaches in the Philippines, or enjoy a nice dinner with a stunning view of the sunset.


Bohol

  • Visit The Philippines third National Geological monument, The Chocolate Hills!
  • Bohol is famous for its Chocolate Hills, hills that turn a chocolate brown during the dry season, making them look like a Hershey's kiss!
